About MindCare

MindCare is a safe, anonymous mental health support platform designed to help individuals share their feelings without judgment. Our chatbot offers compassionate, calming, and non-medical emotional support.

We believe mental health care should be private, accessible, and stigma-free. This platform empowers users to express themselves, find motivation, and receive emotional assistance instantly.

Made for: Students, working professionals, individuals under stress, anyone needing emotional comfort.
